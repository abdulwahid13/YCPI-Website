Our Story

We are a youth-centric NGO with an aim of providing a platform for a shift from aspirations to action for the progression of the youth as an integral part of the society.


Mission

To amplify the voices of the youth and end the marginalsation of the youth in governance, administration and participation


Vision

To make mandera county a friendly environment for the thriving of the youth.

What We Do

We work on the realization of the constitutional dream of breaking the shackles and chains of marginalization from the youth.

Democracy

Engaging the youth in sensitization on the importance of the democratic right to vote as means of impacting change.

Creating Awareness

Creating awareness on the centrality of public participation by the youth on county and national budget making processes.

Civic Education

Providing Civic education on the role the youths play in offering meaningful oversight over the county government


Youth Leadership

Breaking the ceiling of leadership by giving the youth a voice of their own, illuminating the power in their freedom of expression in political leadership


Employment

Providing a platform for a shift from aspirations to action in terms of actualizing the Access to employment opportunities under article 55 of the Constitution


Socio-economic Agenda

Pushing for reforms in the socio-economic agenda under article 21 and 43 of the Constitution.

Where We Work

Our Main Target area is Mandera County.
